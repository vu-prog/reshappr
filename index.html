Questions

Straight answers.

What exactly is the AI follow-up system?
It's an AI agent we install on your lead flow. The moment someone requests an estimate, it texts and calls them, answers their questions about your services and pricing, handles objections, and books them for a site walkthrough. It works your leads around the clock so you're not chasing people down between jobs.
How is this different from a regular CRM or chatbot?
A CRM stores contacts. A chatbot answers FAQs. This is built to convert estimate requests into booked walkthroughs. It follows up via text and phone, handles the back-and-forth, and only surfaces the leads that are ready for you. We also work one contractor per market, so you're not sharing a system with a competitor down the street.
How fast will I see results?
The system goes live within 7 days. From there it works your incoming requests and existing database immediately. Booked walkthroughs can show up in the first two weeks, not two months.
How does the 30-day trial work?
You run it on your real leads for 30 days with no long-term contract. If it isn't booking walkthroughs and earning its cost, you walk away. The trial exists so you can judge it on results.
Do you really only work with one contractor per market?
Yes. We limit it to one contractor per market and cap the program at the first 10 per area. Once your market is claimed, we won't take on a direct competitor in your zip code.
What do I have to do to get it running?
Almost nothing. On the Game Plan Session we collect your services, pricing, and calendar details, then we build, connect, and manage everything. You show up to the walkthroughs it books.
