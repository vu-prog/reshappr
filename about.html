About

Built for remodelers, by someone who actually answers.

Reshappr is a small, founder-led marketing agency for kitchen and bath remodelers. We keep our client list short on purpose, so the work gets real attention.

Why we exist

Most remodelers grow on referrals until the referrals slow down. Then they try paid ads, get a pile of tire-kicker leads, and decide marketing does not work for their business.

The problem is rarely the ads. It is the system around them. Leads that never get followed up. Forms that go to an inbox nobody checks. No clear path from a click to a booked estimate.

Reshappr builds that system and runs it for you. You stay on the job site. We handle the ads, the follow-up, and the pipeline.

Vu Tran Founder, Reshappr
