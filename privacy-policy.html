Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Reshappr LLC ("Reshappr," "we," "us," or "our"). We provide done-for-you marketing services for home improvement and remodeling businesses. We are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website and services. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Full name, phone number, and email address
  • Business details (company name, service area, the type of work you do)
  • Anything you submit through a form, booking, or message on our site

Information collected automatically

  • IP address, browser type and version, and device type
  • Pages visited and activity on our website
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide our marketing services
  • Schedule and confirm strategy calls and consultations
  • Send you reminders, updates, and account notifications
  • Send marketing and promotional messages where you have consented
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as required by law.

3. SMS and Mobile Information

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted. All other categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Mobile information, including phone numbers and SMS consent, will not be shared, sold, rented, or disclosed to third parties for their marketing purposes.

If you opt in to receive text messages from Reshappr, you agree to receive messages such as strategy call confirmations, reminders, account notifications, and marketing or promotional messages. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time, or HELP for help.

4. Your Choices and Opt-Out

  • Text messages: reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe.
  • Email: click the unsubscribe link in any marketing email.
  • Anytime: email us at vu@reshappr.com to be removed.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the site, remember preferences, and measure performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect how parts of the site function.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as needed to provide our services and meet legal and operational requirements. When you opt out of messaging, we suppress your number and email from our marketing lists and retain only the minimum needed to honor your opt-out and comply with the law.

7. How We Protect Your Information

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is fully secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You may request to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal information. To make a request, email us at vu@reshappr.com.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for business owners and are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top reflects the latest version. Continued use of our site and services after changes means you accept the updated policy.
